Introduction, Quick start, Data Summary, Data Download of EVmiRNA

Introduction

EVmiRNA (Extracellular vesicle miRNA expression database) is a comprehensive database including miRNA expression profiles, miRNA related drug, miRNA regulated pathways, miRNA function, and related publications. We analysed 461 small RNA sequencing datasets of EVs from 17 human tissues/diseses.

Quick Start

Browse

  1. EV Samples: Click sources of Exosomes or Microvesicles, and users can browse sample cancer types, heatmap, expressed miRNAs, SRA sample information.

  2. miRNAs list: miRNA basic infomation was presented in this page, and users can click expression detail button to get the epxression histogram. Click the miRNA ID link, users can browsed comprehensive miRNA infomation, contain miRNA basic infomation, miRNA expression profiles, miRNA related drug, miRNA regulated pathways, miRNA function, and related publications.

  3. Specific miRNA: We use SEGtool (SEGtools R packages link and SEGtools reference link) to identify specific miRNAs in each source and cancer. The specific miRNAs maybe potential miRNA biomarkers. Users can browse these miRNAs to help their researches.

Search

  1. Users can search by miRNA, and click search button to get miRNA basic infomation, miRNA expression profiles, miRNA related drug, miRNA regulated pathways, miRNA function, and related publications.

  2. Users can Choose miRNA expression region and Select source types to get miRNA list.

Data Summary

  1. We downloaded all exosomes and microvesicles miRNA samples from NCBI SRA database, after quality control, we obtained 461 samples (4 of these sample were sequenced by ourselves, 1 health control Fibroblasts microvesicles sample and 3 health control Mensenchymal stem cell microvesicles samples.). As below, we presented the sample summary.

    Small RNA sequencing data mapping Strategy

    Datasets with ambiguous description of experiment design and less raw reads were discarded. Subsequently, for each dataset, reads with the multiple N bases (>5) or unexpected length after adapter remove (> 45 nt or < 15 nt) or with low quality (mean quality <= 20) were filtered, and dataset with less reads (< 1M) after the procedure were discarded. Finally, 462 EV datasets were kept for further analysis, which including 225 exosome and 236 MV samples from 17 tissues and 10 cancer types (Table 1). Clean reads were mapped to human pre-miRNA sequences from miRBase V21 (22) by bowtie (23) with 1 mismatch allowed to identify known miRNAs. The sum of all the isoforms mapped to each miRNA was used to calculate the normalized expression profile. RPM (expressed Reads Per Million reads) were employed to assess miRNAs expression profiling and normalize the expression values across samples and conditions.

  2. Annotation data source. miRNA basic information was collected from miRbase. Pathway information was collected from KEGG database, and use Hypergeometric p-value to filter miRNA pathway pairs. Publication information was collected from NCBI pubmed database. miRNA function was collected from NCBI RIF database. miRNA Drug information was collected from SM2miR (Small molecular miRNA database). miRNA targets was from TargetScan, miRanda, TarBase, miRTarBase, miR2Disease, miRecords and miRWalk.
